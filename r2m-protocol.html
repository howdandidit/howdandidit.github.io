What Is R2M

The Foundation
Of The Protocol

R2M stands for Road to Metabolic Health. It's the first and most critical element of Dan's 9-step healing platform — the phase that prepares the body to respond to everything that follows.

Most wellness approaches focus on what to add: supplements, exercise, better food. R2M starts differently. It addresses what needs to be cleared first — specifically toxic load at the cellular level, particularly in visceral and adipose fat cells where environmental toxins accumulate over years.

The 9-day regimen combines six nutritional elements in a specific sequence and pattern. Each element serves a distinct purpose in the overall system. When used together in protocol order, they're designed to work synergistically to support the body's natural clearance processes.

The 9 Days

What Happens
Each Phase

The protocol follows a deliberate three-phase structure. Each phase builds on the last. Skipping ahead or changing the order reduces effectiveness.

Phase 1 · Days 1–2

Prepare & Prime

The body is introduced to the nutritional elements at a lower intensity. This phase supports initial cellular preparation and sets the foundation for what follows.

Phase 2 · Days 3–7

Active Support

The core of the protocol. All six elements are used in full rotation. This is where the primary work happens — supporting the body's natural clearance and fat-mobilization processes.

Phase 3 · Days 8–9

Consolidate & Transition

A gentle wind-down that supports the body in stabilizing results and prepares it for the next phase of Dan's 9-step protocol.
