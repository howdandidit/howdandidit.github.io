Not Your Typical Protein

Not The Gym.

Most people think of protein shakes as a gym supplement — something you use to build muscle after a workout. This protein was designed for a completely different purpose: supporting the body's recovery from metabolic dysfunction.

The key distinction is undenatured whey. Conventional protein processing uses high heat, which destroys the delicate molecular structure of whey — including the precursors needed to produce glutathione, the body's master antioxidant. Undenatured whey preserves those structures intact.

Glutathione declines naturally with age and is depleted by metabolic stress, toxin exposure, and chronic inflammation — exactly the conditions most people in the TDOS framework are experiencing. Replenishing it is not optional. It's foundational.

Why Glutathione Matters

Glutathione is produced in every cell of the body and serves as the primary defense against oxidative stress and cellular damage. Low glutathione levels are associated with accelerated aging, reduced immune function, and impaired detoxification. Most supplements can't raise intracellular glutathione directly — but the precursors in undenatured whey can.
